Why Marc Anthony

Why is Marc Anthony the Best?

Marc Anthony is the top selling tropical salsa artist of all time. He is a four-time Grammy Award and eight-time Latin Grammy Award winner that has sold over 12 million records worldwide. He holds the Guinness World Record for best-selling tropical/salsa artist and the most number-one albums on the Billboard Tropical Albums year-end charts.

With a a 30-year salsa career his distinct vocals have transcended time an kept him relevant. Not only is he an amazing vocalist, he is also an actor that appeared in 11 films and was a recurring character on the show Hawthorne.Marc has had 671 concerts and is currently on his "Viviendo Tour", touring across 2 countries.

Vivir Lo Nuestro

"Vivir Lo Nuestro" is a duet with Marc Anthony and La India. This song was originally released by "Basilio" in 1986 as a ballad and was reimagined into a salsa song in 1993.This collaboration catapulted their salsa careers.

Te Conozco Bien

"Te Conozco Bien" was the first promotional single from Marc's second studio album "Todo a su Tiempo"(1995). The song was awarded the Billboard Latin Music Award in 1996 for "Hot Latin Track of the Year" in the Tropical/Salsa field and was nominated for the Tropical Song of the Year award at the 8th Annual Lo Nuestro Awards in the same year. It was a recipient of the ASCAP Latin Award in 1996.

Y Hubo Alguien

"Y Hubo Alguien" was the first promotional single from Marc's third studio album "Contra La Corriente"(1997)."Y Hubo Alguien" won the Billboard Latin Music Award for "Tropical/Salsa Hot Latin Track of the Year" in 1998 and the Lo Nuestro Award for Tropical Song of the Year in the same year. Its music video was directed by Benny Corral and was nominated in the category of Video of the Year at the 1998 Lo Nuestro Awards.
